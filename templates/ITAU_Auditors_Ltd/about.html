About Us

Established in 2006, we are a leading professional services firm headquartered in Kigali, Rwanda. Our team comprises national and international experts with diverse skills and extensive experience.

We are proud members of the Rwanda Organization of Professional Consultants (ROPC) and the Association of Tax Advisors of Rwanda (ATAR). As a COMPRAC (Community of Practice) member and with accreditation from the National Bank of Rwanda (NBR) for auditing SACCOs and Microfinances, we maintain the highest standards of professional excellence.

Our deep understanding of Rwanda's economic and socio-cultural landscape, as well as our expertise in the Great Lakes region and East African community, enables us to deliver exceptional services in both public and private sectors. We believe that our success is intrinsically linked to the success and satisfaction of our clients.

Our Comprehensive Services Include:

  • Baseline surveys, monitoring, and evaluation of development projects
  • Socio-economic studies and production of gender profiles
  • Audit of SACCOs and Microfinances
  • Accounting, tax, and financial advisory services
  • Development of management tools for accounting and administration
  • Preparation and award examinations in auditing and accounting
  • Creation of procedure manuals
  • Training in auditing, accounting, business, and management
  • Representation and support in tender processes

All our missions are conducted by qualified supervisors, supported by experienced teams. We ensure the highest quality standards throughout each project, providing expert guidance and innovative solutions tailored to our clients' needs.

Our reports adhere to international standards and are available in English and/or French, as per client requirements. With our efficient and flexible approach, creative problem-solving, and commitment to client satisfaction, we bring real added value to every mission we undertake.

Our diverse team of national and international experts brings a wealth of professional experience to each project. We approach each mission on a case-by-case basis, leveraging our in-depth knowledge of various sectors and the socio-economic environment. When needed, we supplement our core team with additional specialists to meet specific project requirements.

Our one and only priority is the customer satisfaction
